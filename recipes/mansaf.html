Mansaf Recipe

The Mansaf is a Jordanian Dish Who Does Not Like Mansaf It Contain smthn the Jordanian People Call "jameed", In the Next Section We ll Give You the Recipe of it

    ingredient

  • 6 pieces of Lamb
  • 1 large yellow onion
  • tablespoon Mansaf Spice Blend
  • 3 Bay Leaves
  • 6 whole cardamom pods,
  • 1 ball of jameed (dried yogurt)
  • 32 ounce plain kefir

Steps To Prepare It :

  1. COOK THE LAMB TO GET MANSAF BROTH
  2. MAKE THE JAMEED (YOGURT SOUP)
  3. MAKE THE RICE
  4. serve Mansaf, the Jordanian way!
