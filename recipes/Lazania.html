Lazania Recipe

Lasagna is the ultimate Italian comfort food. Who doesn't love layers of pasta and cheese? It contains something the Italians call "Bechamel" (or Ricotta in many home versions). In the next section, we'll give you the recipe for it.

    Ingredients

  • 12 Lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb Ground beef
  • 1 jar (24 oz) Marinara sauce
  • 15 oz Ricotta cheese
  • 1 Egg (beaten)
  • 3 cups Shredded mozzarella
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced

Steps To Prepare It:

  1. PREPARE THE SAVORY MEAT SAUCE
  2. MIX THE CHEESE AND HERB FILLING
  3. LAYER THE NOODLES, SAUCE, AND CHEESE
  4. BAKE UNTIL GOLDEN AND BUBBLY!
