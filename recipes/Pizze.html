Pizza Recipe

Pizza is the world's favorite Italian export. Whether thin or thick, it features a crust that Italians call "Cornicione" (the outer rim). In the next section, we'll give you the recipe for a classic Margherita.

    Ingredients

  • 1 lb Pizza dough (fresh or store-bought)
  • 1/2 cup Tomato sauce (San Marzano is best)
  • 8 oz Fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 6-8 Fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tbsp Extra virgin olive oil
  • A pinch of Sea salt

Steps To Prepare It:

  1. STRETCH THE DOUGH TO FORM THE CRUST
  2. SPREAD THE TOMATO SAUCE EVENLY
  3. ADD THE MOZZARELLA AND BASIL LEAVES
  4. BAKE IN A HOT OVEN UNTIL CRISPY!
